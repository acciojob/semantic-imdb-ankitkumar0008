Inception

"Inception" is a science-fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The story follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from deep within the subconscious. He gets a chance to have his criminal record erased if he can perform the impossible — "inception," planting an idea into someone’s mind.
